Our Partners

Paw Print

Paw Print in Bengaluru is dedicated to rescuing and rehabilitating stray animals. They provide medical care, foster homes, and adoption services. The organization also advocates for animal welfare, working to raise awareness and improve the quality of life for stray animals in the community.

Animal care trust

Animal Care Trust, based in Bengaluru, focuses on the rescue, rehabilitation, and care of stray and abandoned animals. They offer medical treatment, foster care, and adoption services. The trust is committed to improving animal welfare and addressing the needs of animals in distress throughout the city.

Feline Foundation

The Feline Foundation is dedicated to the welfare of stray and abandoned cats in Bengaluru. They focus on rescue operations, providing medical care, fostering, and finding suitable homes for cats in need. The foundation also works to educate the public about feline care and adoption.
